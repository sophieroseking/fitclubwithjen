DAIRY, SEASONING AND OTHERS


Dairy

** Needs to be low fat (less than 2g per 100g), high protein (more than 9g per 100g), low in carbs (less than 5g per 100g)

  • Quark
  • High protein low fat Cheese
  • Total 0% fat Greek yoghurt

Seasoning and dressing

** Needs to be low fat (less than 2g per 100g), high protein (more than 9g per 100g), low in carbs (less than 5g per 100g)

  • Daily 2 TABLESPOON tomato or chilli / spicy based sauce
  • Daily 2 tablespoons vinagar or Fresh lemon juice
  • Unlimited fresh Herbs
  • Unlimited salt and sugar free Spices
  • 1 tbsp all seasoning with salt in
  • Cinnamon
  • Garlic
  • Chilli
  • Marmite (HIGH IN SODIUM)
  • Soy sauce (HIGH IN SODIUM)

DO NO ADD ADDITIONAL OILS TO THOSE STATED IN YOUR COOKING OR AS A DRESSING, AS CALORIES ADD UP DO NOT ELIMINATE SODIUM FROM YOUR DIET COMPLETELY. See ‘Sodium and seasoning guidelines’ for more info

Fluids

  • Diet / zero sugar drinks / squash ok in moderation
  • Teas and coffee black ok (if you need to add milk use tiny bit skimmed milk or plant based milk 2 x daily max)

Others

Sugar free chewing gum is fine
Check for hidden calories in your supplements, for example pre workouts
Microwave cooking is acceptable
