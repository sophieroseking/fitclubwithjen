PROTEIN OPTIONS


White
  • Skinless Chicken Breast
  • Turkey Breast
  • Turkey Breast mince
  • White Fish (cod, tilapia, haddock, Pollock etc)
  • King Prawns / prawns / shrimps (add 50g more per 100g white protein)
  • Squid
  • Octopus
  • Scallops
  • Fresh Tuna
  • Tinned tuna (water packed in spring water)
  • 6 egg whites = 100-110g white protein
  • 30g protein powder
  • 150-170g low fat Dairy option (see dairy list)
  • Seitan (homemade 35g Vital Wheat Gluten flour , stock cube & soy sauce)
  • Plain Tofu (fat below 4g per 100g)
Orange
  • Salmon
  • Mackerel – 1 x per week max
  • Herring
  • Sardines
  • Trout
  • Tofu (below 10g fat per 100g )

If you do not like Orange protein just use ‘White’ plus 1 extra TEASPOON ‘healthy’ fats

Red
  • Lean steak
  • Venison
  • Lean Pork loin
  • Lean lamb

All ‘Red’ proteins aim for less than 8g fat per 100g, sausages, burgers, meatballs are not acceptable if high fat content, you may incorporate into your diet if you are macro tracking. Trim fats off meats.

Vegetarian
  • Seitan (homemade 35g Vital Wheat Gluten flour , stock cube & soy sauce)
  • Low fat Cottage Cheese (one portion is small tub av size 200g)
  • Quark (one portion is small tub av size 200g)
  • Total 0% fat greek yoghurt (one portion is small tub 170g)
  • 6 egg whites
  • 1.5 scoops Casein
  • 1.5 scoops Whey / hemp / pea / vegan protein

If you are vegan or vegetarian it is better you macro track your foods. Most vegan protein sources are incomplete, please make sure you understand how to food combine amino acids.
